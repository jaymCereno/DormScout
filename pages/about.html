About

DormScout is the place to go when you need to find a great dorm. It's perfect for college freshmen, transfer students, or anyone helping a friend get closer to campus.

You can easily search for dorms and boarding houses with simple filters to sort by budget, location, and what amenities are available, all without having to sign up or log in. DormScout is designed for students, using just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure everything works smoothly on any device. So, plan wisely and enjoy your living situation.
